# Case Study: Paid Ads Lead Generation Campaign

## Overview
This case study outlines how a paid ads campaign was optimized to improve lead quality and conversion for a service-based business.

---

## Client Situation

- Running ads but getting low-quality leads
- High cost per lead with poor conversion
- No clear funnel strategy

---

## Objective

- Improve lead quality
- Reduce wasted ad spend
- Build a structured lead generation funnel

---

## Strategy Implemented

### 1. Offer Positioning
- Refined messaging to target a specific audience
- Focused on a single core problem
- Highlighted clear outcome in ad copy

---

### 2. Audience Targeting

**Meta Ads:**
- Narrowed interest targeting
- Tested lookalike audiences
- Implemented retargeting

**LinkedIn Ads:**
- Targeted specific job roles
- Focused on relevant industries

---

### 3. Ad Creative Optimization
- Used problem-based hooks
- Simplified ad copy
- Tested multiple creatives

Example Hook:
“Struggling to get high-quality clients from ads?”

---

### 4. Funnel Improvement
- Optimized landing page messaging
- Added stronger CTA
- Improved form structure for better qualification

---

### 5. Testing & Scaling
- Tested 2–3 creatives per audience
- Scaled only winning combinations
- Paused underperforming ads early

---

## Results

- Improved lead quality
- Better conversion from leads to calls
- Reduced wasted ad spend

(Note: Add exact metrics if available)

---

## Key Takeaways

- Offer clarity is more important than ad spend
- Targeting + messaging must align
- Funnel optimization is critical for conversion

---

## Final Insight

Ads amplify strategy—they don’t fix it.

Strong results came from improving the full system, not just running campaigns.
