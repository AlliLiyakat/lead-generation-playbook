# Paid Ads Strategy (Lead Generation)

## Overview
A practical framework to generate high-quality leads using paid ads (Meta & LinkedIn).

---

## Step 1: Offer Clarity
Before running ads, your offer must be clear and specific.

- Who are you targeting?
- What problem are you solving?
- What result are you promising?

Weak offer = poor leads (no matter how good the ads are)

---

## Step 2: Audience Targeting

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)
- Interest-based targeting
- Lookalike audiences (based on leads/customers)
- Retargeting (website visitors, engagement)

### LinkedIn Ads
- Job titles (Founder, Coach, Consultant)
- Industry targeting
- Company size

Start narrow → expand later

---

## Step 3: Ad Creatives

Your creative determines whether people stop scrolling.

### High-performing formats:
- Problem-based hooks
- Short video ads (15–30 sec)
- Direct response copy

### Example Hook:
“Struggling to get high-quality clients from LinkedIn?”

---

## Step 4: Funnel Setup

Traffic alone is useless without a conversion system.

### Basic Funnel:
Ad → Landing Page → Lead Form → Call Booking

### Landing Page Essentials:
- Clear headline
- Pain-point focused copy
- Social proof (if available)
- Strong CTA

---

## Step 5: Lead Quality Optimization

Not all leads are equal.

To improve quality:
- Refine targeting
- Improve messaging
- Add qualification questions in forms

---

## Step 6: Testing & Scaling

Start with small tests:
- 2–3 creatives
- 1–2 audiences

Then scale:
- Increase budget on winning ads
- Kill low-performing campaigns

---

## Common Mistakes
- Running ads without a clear offer
- Targeting too broad
- Ignoring landing page conversion
- Scaling too fast without testing

---

## Final Takeaway

Ads don’t fix bad strategy.

Strong results come from:
Offer + Targeting + Creative + Funnel + Optimization
